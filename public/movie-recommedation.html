The Best Movies According to Harsh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

This is a great movie with the best thriller story. I love the integrity of Protagonist.

Swades

It is about an NRI guy who comes to India for a purpose but stay back after seeing the hardships of people and try to do its best for the betterment of the society.

Shutter Island

It is a worth watching movie if you love thriller and mystery movie as it keeps you engaged till the end.
